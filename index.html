Yue Wu is a Ph.D. candidate in the Division of Applied Mathematics at Brown University under the supervision of Prof. Chi-Wang Shu. His main research interests include numerical analysis and scientific computing. He grew up in Wuxi, a beautiful city in eastern China. Outside academia, he is an aviation enthusiast and enjoys road cycling, hiking, and road trips.
